## Pizza Hawaiana

Solution 
1. Purchase the ingredients at Pricesmart
2. Make the dough
3. Knead the dough
4. Roll out the dough in a circular shape
5. Add pizza sauce 
6. Add cheese
7. Add ham
6. Add pineapple chunks over the top
7. Bake it for 12 to 15 minutes 
8. Enjoy it while you watch Netflix
